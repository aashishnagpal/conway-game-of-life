Instructions
  • Enter number of rows and columns in respective text boxes and press CREATE NEW BOARD to generate an empty Game of Life Board.
  • Click on any number of cells in the generated board to create the initial state.
  • Click on NEXT to get the next generation from current state of life.
  • Click Auto Advance to toggle automatic generations of newer generations.
  • Click on RESET to go back and create a fresh board.
  • DO NOT Refresh the page or use Back button on the browser.
